Hair Transplant

Istanbul: The World Capital of Hair Transplantation

Istanbul performs more hair transplant procedures than any other city in the world, attracting hundreds of thousands of patients each year from Europe, the Middle East, and beyond. The combination of world-renowned surgeons, state-of-the-art facilities, and highly competitive pricing has made Istanbul the undisputed global leader in hair restoration.

Choice Clinic partners with the top hair transplant clinics and surgeons in Istanbul to ensure every patient receives exceptional care and natural, permanent results. Our medical team uses the latest technology and techniques, including micro-motor FUE and the Choi implanter pen, to deliver dense, undetectable hairlines tailored to each patient's unique facial structure.

From the moment you reach out, a dedicated English-speaking coordinator guides you through every step of your journey. Our all-inclusive packages cover your procedure, hotel accommodation, VIP airport and clinic transfers, medication, and comprehensive aftercare, so you can focus entirely on your transformation without worrying about logistics.

Our Procedures

Hair Transplant Techniques

Advanced methods for natural, lasting results

FUE Hair Transplant

Follicular Unit Extraction for natural, scarless results. Individual follicles are harvested and implanted with precision, achieving up to 5,000 grafts in a single session.

DHI Hair Transplant

Direct Hair Implantation using the Choi pen allows simultaneous channel opening and follicle placement, delivering maximum density and precision with faster recovery.

Beard Transplant

Fill in patchy areas or create a full, well-defined beard with natural-looking results. Follicles are carefully placed to match your natural growth direction and density.

Eyebrow Transplant

Restore or enhance your eyebrows with precise follicle placement. Each graft is angled to replicate the natural brow pattern for seamless, lasting results.

Women's Hair Transplant

Specialized techniques tailored for female pattern hair loss. Targeted restoration of thinning areas while preserving your existing hair and natural parting.

PRP Hair Therapy

Platelet-rich plasma therapy harnesses your body's own growth factors to stimulate hair follicles, strengthen existing hair, and accelerate healing after transplant procedures.
