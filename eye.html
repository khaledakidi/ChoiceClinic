Our Procedures

Eye Surgery Treatments

Tailored solutions for every vision need, from laser correction to premium lens implants

LASIK Surgery

Laser-assisted correction for nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. LASIK reshapes the cornea using a precise femtosecond laser, delivering rapid visual improvement with minimal discomfort and a recovery measured in hours, not days.

Learn More

PRK Surgery

Surface laser treatment ideal for patients with thin corneas or those not suited for LASIK. PRK delivers excellent long-term results by gently reshaping the corneal surface, making it a proven and safe alternative for lasting vision correction.

Learn More

Smart Lens (Trifocal)

Premium intraocular lens replacement for clear vision at all distances — near, intermediate, and far. Smart Lens technology eliminates dependence on glasses and is an excellent solution for patients with presbyopia or early-stage cataracts.

Learn More

ICL (Implantable Lens)

Implantable contact lens designed for high prescriptions where LASIK is not suitable. The ICL is placed behind the iris and in front of the natural lens, providing exceptional visual quality while being completely reversible if needed.

Learn More
