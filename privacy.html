Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Introduction

Choice Clinic ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal data you provide to us when using our website or services. By using our website or submitting your information, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information when you interact with our website or contact us:

  • Personal details — your name, email address, phone number, and country of residence
  • Medical information — medical history, treatment preferences, and health-related details shared through our consultation forms
  • Travel information — passport details and travel dates necessary for coordinating your visit to Istanbul
  • Website usage data — information collected through cookies and analytics tools, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide personalized consultations
  • To arrange and coordinate medical consultations with our partner clinics and hospitals
  • To coordinate travel logistics, hotel accommodations, and airport transfers
  • To send you updates, follow-up information, and promotional communications (only with your consent)
  • To improve our website, services, and overall patient experience
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our legitimate interests

Data Sharing

We share your personal information only as necessary to deliver the services you have requested. Specifically:

  • We share relevant medical and personal details with our partner clinics and hospitals in Istanbul solely for the purpose of treatment coordination and planning
  • We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us with website hosting, analytics, and communication tools
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes
  • We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our legal rights

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect it, including:

  • Industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for data transmitted through our website
  • Secure servers with restricted access controls
  • Regular security reviews and updates to our systems
  • Staff training on data protection and confidentiality practices

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to maintaining the highest practical standards.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw your consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Data portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@choiceclinic.net. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and to collect analytical data about site usage. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website.

  • Essential cookies — required for basic site functionality such as navigation and form submissions
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve our content and services

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
