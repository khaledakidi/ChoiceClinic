About Face Aesthetic
Expert Facial Surgery in Istanbul

Istanbul has become one of the world's leading destinations for facial aesthetic surgery, combining internationally trained, board-certified surgeons with modern hospital infrastructure at a fraction of the cost you would find in Western Europe or the United States. At Choice Clinic, we connect you with Turkey's most respected facial surgeons who specialise in delivering natural, harmonious results tailored to each patient's unique anatomy and goals.

Every procedure begins with an in-depth consultation where your surgeon analyses your facial proportions, discusses your expectations, and designs a personalised surgical plan. Our facilities are equipped with the latest imaging and surgical technology, and our clinical teams follow rigorous safety protocols to ensure the highest standard of care throughout your journey.

With Choice Clinic, your experience extends beyond the operating room. We coordinate your entire trip including flights, premium hotel accommodation, private airport transfers, and a dedicated patient coordinator who stays with you from arrival to departure and beyond.
