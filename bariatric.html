Our Procedures

Bariatric Surgery Options

Personalised surgical and non-surgical solutions to help you achieve lasting weight loss and improved health.

Gastric Sleeve

Also known as sleeve gastrectomy, this procedure removes approximately 80% of the stomach, creating a smaller tube-shaped stomach. Performed using minimally invasive laparoscopic technique, it significantly reduces appetite and promotes substantial, long-term weight loss with a relatively short recovery period.

Gastric Balloon

A non-surgical, temporary solution where a soft silicone balloon is placed in the stomach via endoscopy. The balloon reduces stomach capacity and helps patients feel full sooner, promoting controlled weight loss over a 6 to 12-month period. Ideal for patients seeking a less invasive approach to kick-start their weight loss journey.

Get a Quote

Gastric Bypass

Roux-en-Y gastric bypass reroutes the digestive system by creating a small stomach pouch and connecting it directly to the small intestine. This dual mechanism restricts food intake and reduces calorie absorption, delivering maximum weight loss results. It is one of the most effective long-term solutions for severe obesity.

Get a Quote

Mini Gastric Bypass

A simplified version of the traditional gastric bypass using a single anastomosis (connection) between the stomach and intestine. This streamlined technique delivers highly effective weight loss results with a shorter operative time, reduced complexity, and faster recovery compared to the standard Roux-en-Y procedure.
