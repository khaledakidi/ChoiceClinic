Why Istanbul

World-Class Dental Care at Choice Clinic

Choice Clinic partners with JCI-accredited dental facilities in Istanbul to deliver exceptional results for international patients. Our network of experienced dental specialists, each with over 10 years of clinical expertise, perform thousands of procedures annually using the latest digital dentistry technology including 3D scanning, CAD/CAM design, and guided implant surgery.

We exclusively use premium European implant brands including Straumann (Switzerland) and Nobel Biocare (Sweden), ensuring the highest standards of biocompatibility and longevity for every patient. From single implants to full-mouth rehabilitations, every treatment is backed by international warranties and comprehensive aftercare.

Our all-inclusive dental packages cover everything from airport transfers and luxury hotel accommodation to dedicated English-speaking coordinators who guide you through every step. With savings of up to 70% compared to UK and US dental costs, Choice Clinic makes world-class dental care accessible without compromising on quality, materials, or patient safety.
